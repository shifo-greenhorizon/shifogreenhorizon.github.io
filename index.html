The Corporate Vision

The Dawn of a New
Agricultural Standard

"Inspired by the horizon of a rising sun, Shifo GreenHorizon represents the future of regional food security, modern agribusiness, and sustainable land development."

We are transforming 110 hectares of fertile land into a high-yield, technologically advanced agricultural powerhouse. By combining scalable open-field farming with protected cultivation infrastructure, we are bridging the gap between traditional agriculture and modern, climate-resilient crop production.

Our four-tier crop architecture — from ultra-fast vegetable cycles generating immediate cash flow, through rapid tropical income crops, to premium long-term orchard assets — ensures every hectare earns from day one while building lasting export-market dominance.

Project Scale & Infrastructure

Operational Metrics & Infrastructure Highlights

Every figure below represents a committed, engineered operational target — not a projection.

🌍

Total Project Footprint

110 Hectares of strategically positioned agricultural land earmarked for phased, high-value development — 10 Ha currently producing, 100 Ha ready for immediate expansion.

🏗️

Protected Cultivation

Engineered infrastructure featuring a planned layout of 50 to 70 modern high-tunnel greenhouses, optimised for premium quality, climate resilience, and ultra-fast vegetable cycle production.

🌿

Commercial Cash Crops

10 hectares dedicated to high-demand papaya cultivation (4 harvest seasons annually, 400–560 kg per tree per year), alongside high-yield regional produce including tomatoes and cucumbers for fast-food supply chains.

♻️

Sustainable Ecosystem

Designed to integrate eco-friendly farming methodologies — freshwater aquaculture, apiculture, livestock, and closed-loop fertigation — delivering efficient resource management and long-term economic stability.
