Integrated Agribusiness

Beyond the Orchard:
A Full Ecosystem

Livestock, fish, and bees turn farm waste into revenue and farm inputs into profit — a closed-loop system that dramatically reduces external supply costs and adds multiple income streams.

🐑

Sheep & Goats

Grazing in cleared orchard alleys keeps vegetation down at zero cost. Manure is processed into organic compost for fertigation.

🐓

Poultry

Chickens provide pest control in vegetable zones, eggs for local sale, and high-nitrogen litter for soil amendment.

🐟

Freshwater Aquaculture

On-farm fish ponds turn surplus irrigation water into a protein crop. Nutrient-rich pond water cycles back into the drip fertigation system.

🍯

Apiculture (Bees)

Beehives drive pollination efficiency across all fruit tree tiers. Honey production adds a premium branded product line for local and export markets.
