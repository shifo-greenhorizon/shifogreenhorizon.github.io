Integrated Agribusiness

Beyond the Orchard:
A Full Ecosystem

Livestock, fish, bees, and people turn farm waste into revenue and farm inputs into profit — a closed-loop system that dramatically reduces external supply costs and adds multiple income streams, fully aligned with the master plan's integrated sectors.

🐑

Sheep & Goats

Rotational livestock units, scaled to orchard floor capacity, free-roam under tree canopies. Grazing keeps vegetation down at zero cost; manure is processed into organic compost for fertigation.

🐓

Poultry

Chickens integrated into the same rotational system provide pest control in vegetable zones, eggs for local sale, and high-nitrogen litter for soil amendment via continuous managed collection.

🐟

Freshwater Aquaculture

1 Ha of engineered retention ponds stocked with 25,000–30,000 Tilapia and Catfish fingerlings. Nutrient-rich pond water cycles directly into the greenhouse drip fertigation system as free fertiliser.

🍯

Apiculture (Bees)

100–150 managed beehives drive pollination efficiency across all fruit tree tiers, lifting farm-wide fruit set by up to 30%. Honey production adds a premium branded product line for local and export markets.

🌱

Social Impact Programme

Targeted local training placements give young men and women ongoing agricultural skill certification and field leadership experience across every harvest season — qualifying the estate for international ESG grants.
